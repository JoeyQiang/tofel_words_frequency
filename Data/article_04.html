University, as a dreaming place for some people, plays many important roles to a person’s life. 
Since many countries invest huge amount of money into university education, there must be reasons that going to college is helpful either for person, family or society. Therefore, the author will examine several points below to express ideas.
 
First of all, studying knowledge is imperative for a person and university undoubtedly provides the good place for studying. 
After four years hard working, many students acquire a skill which helps them to find a job after graduation. 
Furthermore, these people can earn salary to make ends meet and even support their family. 
Of course, going to university is not only helpful for a person on economic aspect, but also helpful on spirit aspect. 
University education have the ability to form a person’s value system about himself, family and society, which maybe is one of the most important thing that a person should figure out through all his life. 
 
The second point is that education background is a fundamental certificate for a person to make up a family.
It doesn’t mean to look down those people who don’t have college degree. 
It’s just a fact that everyone should know about his or her future companion. 
For instance, when a woman is looking for a husband, she has a high probability to look for a guy who received a college degree, which not only shows a person’s study ability but also shows a person’s thinking ability. 
After all, a couple needs to live with each other everyday, which means they should have the similar knowledge background.
 
Another reason is due to the society effect. 
We all know that university is the fertile soil to cultivate politicians, scientists, and writers, who will make powerful influence to the world. 
These genius people will create comprehensive regulations, advanced technologies and thoughtful books. 
These material or spiritual things bring uncountable values for the current generation and even the future generations. 
In order to build a better future, the society definitely needs university to take the educational responsibility.
 
Broadly speaking, I strongly support people to go to university because the three reasons I mentioned above. 
Nonetheless, whether go to university or not is a personal choice, and we shouldn’t deny the right to those people who say no to university because of variety of personal reasons.
