If someone ask me that should a factory be built in your community, my answer will be definitely no. 
Not only my subject hunch tells me it is not a smart idea, but also my object logic persuades me to oppose the suggestion. 
I will further illustrate my reasons below.

First of all, a factory will produce pollution to the environment. 
Needless to say that there is almost no factories produce zero harmful substance to the environment. 
Even though a factory might increase the local economic situation, the history has told us that abandoning environment to compensate for economic development is a silly idea. 
It is not a progressive and continuing development plan. 
After all, the final goal of a city is to provide a better living place for people. 
When environmental destruction happens, it may be too late to spend multiple times of money to recover the environment.

Another important reason is the risk of over-population. 
As is known to all that a factory can attract labors. 
When an increasing number of workers go to a city, especially if the city has no plan for that, it might be a disaster for local people. 
For example, there might not be enough parking lots. 
At rush-hours, traffic jam always happens.
And the rising house price and the rising food price will also come. 
Even worse, maybe the newborn children don’t have enough schools to study, which is the last thing we want to see.

Moreover, the factory might change people’s lifestyle. 
Suppose you used to live in a quiet and cosy town, but suddenly a noisy factory comes into your life. 
And both your classmates and relatives work for the factory due to the high salary.
Therefore, your parents also want you to work for the factory but you haven’t got ready yet.
You will have a mental pressure and cognitive dissonance. 
You are expected to do something that you don’t want to do, which I call lifestyle change. 
After all, it is always not easy to say goodbye to yesterday.

Generally speaking, I disagree to build a factory in my community. 
Admittedly, there are several advantages to build a factory in local, such as improving salary and increasing city tax revenues. 
But after comparing disadvantages with advantages, a factory is not the thing that I want to see to appear around my neighborhood.
