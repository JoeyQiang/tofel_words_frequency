Though there is a famous saying that books contain gold and beauties, I still prefer that experiences are more important than books. 
I wouldn’t under-evaluate the value of books. 
Otherwise, intelligent people won’t spend such a huge time on studying knowledge from books. 
However, from the aspect of importance to people’s life, experiences have unique and irreplaceable significance. 
I would like to further illustrate my reasons as follows. 

First of all, personal experiences, compared with books, are more practical. 
What are books? In my opinion, books are valuable experiences that are summarized by previous people. 
In another words, books actually have no essential difference from personal experiences because they are all experiences. 
Nonetheless, the difference is that books contain other people’s experiences, not yours. 
As we all known that without putting into practice, knowledge is almost useless. 
Besides, you never know whether other people’s experiences are suitable for your situation. 
Therefore, only personal experiences are your most loyal partner. 

Another important reason is that experiences can bring people exact feelings which books cannot provide. 
For example, you are keen to know more about Tibet and then you have two options. 
Option one is to read a bunch of books related to Tibet. 
And option two is taking a tour in Tibet for several months. 
I strongly believe the option two will leave you much deeper impression, which helps us better understand the meaning of life, such as happiness, sadness and anger. 
And knowing the meaning of life can lead us to uncountable possibilities.

Moreover, as human beings, courage is the ultimate power to push us move forward when we face overwhelming challenges. 
Suppose you want to challenge yourself to finish a goal that you have failed once, and after long time hard working, you make it. 
How is your feeling? 
I assume you feel confident and your heart is filled with courage. 
More important, for the next challenge, you will be brave enough to overcome it as well. 
This is the point I want to mention that experiences can give us courage, which is like the beacon light for ships in the dark ocean.

In the end, I firmly believe that experiences are more important than books due to its practicability, feelings and life meanings. 
And I encourage everyone to try varies experience when you feel life is dull. 
You will feel life is more colorful than ever if you feel it by heart.
