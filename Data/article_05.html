There is no doubt that parents are life-long teachers for their children. 
Parents spend whatever they can to provide a better living condition to their kids. 
With children growing up, parents get older day by day but the love never fades.
I would like to say that parents are the people who love their children the best. 
However, when the topic comes to “are parents are best teachers”, my opinion is no.

First of all, parents are almost only people who accompany with their children before they go to school. 
The time duration is so long that children have acquired many characters which are duplicated from their parents. 
For example, children will mimic the behaviors from parents. 
Unfortunately, not every parents are good enough and some of them have bad influence. 
On this condition, it is necessary to have the right person to teach the child to do the right thing, and that is why the society needs school to play the extra educational role.

What’s more, teachers are the people to pass on variety of knowledge. 
However, every parents have their own limitations in different aspects.
It makes no sense to say that parents are best in every field. 
Therefore, in order to help children study most efficiently, parents should look for more qualified people as teachers for their kids. 
And I believe parents already know this fact otherwise they won’t pay expensive fee to hire good teachers to teach children piano, dance, math, etc,.

Finally, parents will eventually pass away someday and they cannot help children forever. 
As children, our life have only a certain time overlapped with our parents. 
And I believe my parents must want me to take care of myself well even though one day they have to leave me earlier. 
Therefore, personally speaking, people should exert every energy to be the best teacher of their own because your best teachers, if you believe they were your parents, want you to do so.

Admittedly, the most generous love in the world is parents’ love, which causes long-duration and huge influence to their children. 
Of course, I sincerely hope parents are all best teachers, but rationally speaking, I think parents are only influential teachers but are not best.
